The Gospels

Gospel of Matthew

The Gospel of Matthew presents Jesus as the fulfillment of Old Testament prophecies. It begins with the genealogy of Jesus and emphasizes his role as the Messiah.



Gospel of Mark

The Gospel of Mark is fast-paced and focuses on the actions of Jesus. Mark portrays Jesus as a servant leader, emphasizing his miracles and teachings.



Gospel of Luke

Luke's Gospel emphasizes the compassion of Jesus, especially toward the poor and marginalized. It is known for detailed accounts of Jesus' birth and parables.



Gospel of John

John's Gospel is more theological, focusing on the divine nature of Jesus. It highlights Jesus' relationship with God the Father and his role as the Word made flesh.
