Bible Characters

Key figures who shaped the history of the Bible

Moses

Moses was the great lawgiver and leader who led the Israelites out of slavery in Egypt and delivered God's commandments to the people.



David

David, the second king of Israel, is known for his deep faith, his defeat of Goliath, and his authorship of many Psalms.



Abraham

Abraham, known as the father of faith, made a covenant with God, and his faith was credited to him as righteousness. He was promised descendants as numerous as the stars.



Paul

Paul, originally known as Saul, was a persecutor of Christians who converted after encountering Jesus. He became one of the most influential apostles, writing much of the New Testament.



Mary, Mother of Jesus

Mary, the mother of Jesus, is revered for her obedience to God's will and for giving birth to the Savior of the world.
