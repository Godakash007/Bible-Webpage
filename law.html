Law in the Bible

The foundation of the biblical law and history

Genesis

Genesis covers the creation of the world, the fall of man, the flood, and the story of the Patriarchs such as Abraham, Isaac, Jacob, and Joseph.



Exodus

Exodus focuses on the deliverance of Israel from Egypt, the giving of the Ten Commandments, and the establishment of the Mosaic covenant.



Leviticus

This book contains laws regarding worship, offerings, and moral purity.
